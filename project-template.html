Decentralized UAV Swarm Chain Formation

This project focused on developing a decentralized control algorithm for a swarm of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The goal was to enable the swarm to form and maintain a chain formation without a central command unit, relying only on local communication between neighboring drones. This enhances robustness and scalability for tasks like surveillance and environmental monitoring.
